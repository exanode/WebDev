Sachin's Top 3 Movies of all time

October Sky

Jake Gyllenhall stars as Homer Hickam, one of the rocket boys. This is a movie about how a group of kids from West Virginia win a science fair against all odds.

A Beautiful Mind

Russel Crowe as John Nash, a wonderful movie about how a gifted but different mathematician and his amazing journey through his adult life.

The Shawshank Redemption

The #2 highest rated movie on iMDB. This movie is a story about Andy Dufrense and how some birds' wings are too colorful for a cage.
